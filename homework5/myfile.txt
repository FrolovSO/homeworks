Criminal Code of the Republic of Belarus
Article 130. Inciting racial, national, religious or other social hatred or discord
1. Intentional actions aimed at inciting racial, national, religious or other social enmity or discord on the basis of racial, national, religious, linguistic or other social affiliation, -

shall be punishable by a fine, or arrest, or restraint of liberty for a term up to five years, or imprisonment for the same term.
